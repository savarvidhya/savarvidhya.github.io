About Us

Savar Vidhya is an International organization for foreign language trainings, State boards/CBSE after school tuition classes. We have team across the globe which makes us stand out from the crowd. Our offices are being operated from Magdeburg (Germany), Jamnagar (Gujrat) & Jaipur (Rajasthan).

Savar State Boards/ CBSE Savar Vidhya reaches to the end learners through its unique teaching methods which we gained through our experience, doing deep research and perfect analysis of rural educational environment. Savar Vidhya is ready to hold the hands of those individuals who even do not know how to take online classes. We mentor them and understand their goal. Our research shows that students are more comfortable learning in their own language for example if student is from Punjab then Savar Vidhya is ready to coach them using Punjabi language. Same concept applies for all the regional languages. We are the vernacular teaching platform which is helping our students to learn in their own language.

Our Mission

State boards after school tuition: Our detailed research had shocking results that most of the students from rural area are having limited resources to learn. They do not even connected with the latest technologies used for learning. The knowledge of English language is also a barrier for them but as we are aware that language is just a medium of communication. If work upon this barrier then we may have a drastic change in rural talent. Savar Vidhya has decided to give daily Spoken free classes to all the students. This will surely help them to connect with world and dream high. Further to Spoken English classes Savar Vidhya team is always ready to boost their school syllabus learning through our after school classes for all the subjects in their local language. This is this reason we are considered as vernacular organization.
We are also offering them foreign language classes which is definitely triggering their interest about the future opportunities in foreign countries, foreign inventions, international ideas to do something new. Savar Vidhya is connected with every individual even in the remote areas of India.
We believe that Money should not stop the learning hence are firmly stand with our students and offer them world class coaching classes.
We not only train our students in German but also we help them achieve their end goal like getting a Job in India or in Germany, migration to Germany for higher education & very good performance in Goethe, Telc, International Universities & schools.
We have a team of Native /Non Native faculties which increases the opportunity to learn more from more than one faculty.
